Math Practice Problems

Problem 1 – Ratios & Proportions:
A recipe calls for 3 cups of flour to make 24 cookies. How many cups of flour are needed for 40 cookies?
Problem 2 – Area of Composite Figures:
A rectangle 10 cm by 6 cm has a triangle cut out from one corner with base 4 cm and height 5 cm. Find the remaining area.
Problem 3 – Probability:
A bag has 5 red, 3 blue, and 2 green marbles. What is the probability of drawing a blue marble?
Problem 4 – Integer Operations:
Evaluate: –8 + 12 – (–5) × 2
