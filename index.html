Algebra Practice

Problem 1 – Systems of Equations:
Solve: 3x + 2y = 13
        x – 4y = –11
(Use substitution or elimination method)
Problem 2 – Quadratic Factoring:
Factor completely: x² – 7x + 12 = 0
Problem 3 – Slope & Equations:
Find the equation of the line passing through (–2, 5) with slope 3/4.

Geometry Foundations Practice

Problem 1 – Triangle Angles:
In △ABC, ∠A = 65° and ∠B = 45°. Calculate ∠C.
Problem 2 – Pythagorean Theorem:
A ladder 17 ft long leans against a wall. The base is 8 ft from the wall. How high up the wall does the ladder reach?
Problem 3 – Distance Formula:
Calculate the distance between points A(1, –3) and B(6, 5).

Logic & Reasoning Practice

Problem 1 – Deductive Reasoning:
All even numbers are divisible by 2. 14 is even. Conclusion?
Problem 2 – Probability:
A spinner has 5 equal sections: red, blue, green, yellow, purple. What is P(landing on red or blue)?
Problem 3 – Logical Conclusion:
If it rains, the ground is wet. The ground is wet. Is it necessarily raining? Explain.
